Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The NIHC League ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and store your personal data when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Player names and contact details
  • Team registration information
  • Email addresses submitted via forms
  • Participation and league activity data
  • Basic website usage data (analytics if used)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To register players and teams
  • To manage league fixtures and competitions
  • To communicate updates and important information
  • To improve our services and website

3. Legal Basis

We process your data based on consent, legitimate interest in running the league, and where necessary to fulfil participation in league activities.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

Data may be shared with league organisers, officials, and trusted service providers (such as website hosting platforms) where necessary to operate the league.

5. Data Storage & Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data, including secure hosting, limited access to administrative accounts, and encrypted connections (HTTPS).

6. Data Retention

We only retain personal data for as long as necessary to manage the league and comply with legal obligations.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
